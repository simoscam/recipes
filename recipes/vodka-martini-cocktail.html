Vodka Martini Cocktail

Description

The martini is one of the most well-known mixed alcoholic drinks. Ian Fleming's James Bond is famously known for ordering it "skaken, not stirred".

Ingredients

  • vodka
  • dry vermouth
  • olives

Steps

  1. Combine the vodka and the dry vermouth in a mixing glass with ice.
  2. Shake until chilled, then strain into a chilled martini glass.
  3. Top the coctail with olives threaded onto a toothpick.
