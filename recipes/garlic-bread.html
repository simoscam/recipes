Garlic Bread

Description

Garlic bread is usually served as an entree or a side dish. It consist of bread topped with garlic and olive oil or butter, optionally with other herbs as well.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons of grated parmesan cheese
  • 1 teaspoon of garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon of dried, crushed basil
  • 2 baguettes, split
  • 2 tablespoons of softened margarine

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 200 degrees Celsius.
  2. Stir together the parmesan cheese, garlic powder and the basil in a small bowl, then set it aside.
  3. Spread baguettes with margarine, then sprinkle the garlic-cheese mixture on top. Put the baguettes onto a baking sheet.
  4. Bake them in the preheated oven for about 10 minutes, until golden brown.
